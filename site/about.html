AboutIndependence

An honest read in a category built on noise

bestCXreviews is an independent, analyst-style publication for AI software that helps companies manage customer conversations. Our entire value is that we owe nothing to the vendors we cover.

Why we exist

The market for AI conversation tools is exploding and almost impossible to read clearly. Every vendor claims to deflect most tickets and resolve issues autonomously. Most published comparisons are affiliate bait or vendor-sponsored. We are the opposite: a credible, opinionated guide that cuts through the hype around AI agents, copilots, and conversation automation, and tells practitioners what actually works.

Who it is for

  • CX, support, and customer success leaders evaluating AI agents or copilots.
  • Support and CS operations roles who own the tooling and the metrics.
  • Founders and product leaders building or buying their conversation stack.

Vendors and investors read us too, for a clear-eyed view of the category. That is fine. They just do not get to shape what we say.

What we stand for

An honest read on what AI can and cannot do for customer conversations right now.
  • Independent, always. Recommendations are defensible in public.
  • Evidence over adjectives. Claims come with receipts: transcripts, numbers, screenshots.
  • Plain-spoken. Short sentences, real words, no buzzword soup.
  • Opinionated, with reasoning shown. We take a position and explain how we got there.

How we make money, in full

Independence is the product, so we are specific about revenue. Here is the complete list of what we allow, and what we refuse.

We allow:

  • Clearly labeled sponsorships in the newsletter, fully separated from editorial. A sponsor never sees a review before publication and never influences a verdict.
  • Premium benchmark reports comparing AI agent performance over time, sold to readers.
  • A reader-supported membership.
  • Affiliate links only where disclosed, and only on tools that earned their place on the merits. If an affiliate relationship and a verdict ever conflict, the verdict wins and the link goes.

We refuse:

  • Pay-to-play rankings or paid placements.
  • Vendor approval over any review.
  • Any revenue model that compromises the independence the brand is built on.

Contact

Story tips, corrections, and vendor briefings are all welcome at hello@bestcxreviews.com. We correct errors openly and quickly. If you think a verdict is wrong, send evidence and we will publish what we find.
