04

Voice & tone

We earn trust the hard way: by being useful, specific, and unafraid to disagree with the market.
01

Independent, always

We answer to readers, not vendors. Every recommendation can be defended in public, and we say how we make money.

We say: "Strong for high-volume billing questions, weak on nuanced complaints." Not: "A leading, best-in-class solution."
02

Evidence over adjectives

Claims come with receipts: transcripts, numbers, screenshots. If we cannot show it, we do not assert it.

We say: "Resolved 31 of 40 test cases without escalation." Not: "Incredibly powerful AI that transforms support."
03

Plain-spoken

We respect the reader's intelligence and their time. Short sentences. Real words. No buzzword soup.

We say: "It hands off to a human when it is unsure." Not: "Leverages agentic orchestration to optimize CX."
04

Opinionated, with care

We take a position. We also show our reasoning, so a reader who disagrees knows exactly where we parted ways.

We say: "Most 'autonomous' claims do not survive a hard edge case." Not: "It depends, every tool has pros and cons."
